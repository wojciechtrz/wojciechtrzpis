Motorola Solutions

Partner Hub - sales platform connecting partners and customers directly for streamlined orders, pricing and support.

My role

As a Senior UX Designer, I had the privilege of serving as the Lead Designer for the Partner Hub sales platform, where I crafted an intuitive and user-centric experience. Additionally, I led the design efforts for the Motorola Solutions system.

Partner Hub Dashboard

Partner Hub Dashboard serves as a management tool for account and sales oversight, displaying billing details, order statuses, user administration, and open cases. It provides real-time data on invoices, order summaries with a 100-order overview, and customizable links for quick access to key resources

Partner Hub Modules

Partner Hub is a highly modular design, where different user types can access specific modules like Billing & Invoices, Order Summary, User Administration, and Open Cases.

Workspace Customization

Each section is independently accessible and customizable, allowing users to focus on relevant tasks, rearrange layouts, and add personalized links. This flexibility empowers users to tailor their workspace to individual roles and workflows.
