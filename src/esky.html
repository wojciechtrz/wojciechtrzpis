My role

Collaborated within a cross-functional product team to design and improve the web application. Responsible for designing the Deals section of the platform, focusing on engaging layouts and intuitive navigation. Created concepts and layouts for the Home page.

eSky Deals page

The Deals page is designed to make discovering and booking discounted flights and travel packages simple and engaging. It help users quickly find relevant offers supporting spontaneous travel decisions.

We tested multiple design variants of the Deals page, including grid vs list layouts, filter placements and card styles.

Working on eSky.pl gave me valuable experience designing for a global e-commerce platform, balancing complex business requirements with user needs, and creating digital products that make travel planning easier and more enjoyable experience.
