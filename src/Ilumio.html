iLumio Smart TV

Interactive in-room TV system delivering entertainment, hotel services and personalized guest experiences all on the big screen.

My role

Designed the iLumio smart TV application tailored for hotel environments, enhancing guest engagement. Produced animations and visual concepts from low-fidelity sketches to high-fidelity interactive prototypes

iLumio App – Main View

The main view of the iLumio app is designed for quick access to essential hotel services and features. Allows guests to browse and play TV channels, movies, or music directly from their device. Provides access to hotel services like room service, housekeeping requests, spa reservations, or maintenance reports. Enables communication between guests and hotel staff for inquiries or special requests.

Media Selection Screen

iLumio app serves as a media selection hub, allowing users to quickly choose from popular streaming services and entertainment apps.

Live TV Channel View

Live TV viewing interface iintegrates immersive full-screen video playback delivers essential context—such as current program details, upcoming shows, and quick access to interactive features—without disrupting the viewing experience.

The iLumio mobile app extends the hotel guest experience beyond the TV screen, providing a comprehensive suite of services directly on users’ personal devices.

The iLumio app uses a modern, minimalist icon set designed for clarity and elegance. Icons are typically line-based or solid, in white or light shade.
